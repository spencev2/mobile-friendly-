Movement Madness

Stay aggressive, stay mobile, and use movement tools constantly. This challenge is all about speed, pressure, and making yourself hard to predict.

Submit Your Run Back to Challenges

Challenge Overview

Instead of playing static or safe, you are expected to keep momentum and use mobility as part of your playstyle in fights, rotations, and pressure situations.

Rules

Carry movement items whenever possible.

Use movement as part of fights instead of saving it forever.

Play with constant pressure and active repositioning.

Best Strategy

Use speed and unpredictability to control the fight. Quick repositioning can help you escape danger, take better angles, and keep opponents guessing.

What Makes It Hard

It is easy to overextend when playing fast. This challenge rewards controlled aggression, not reckless pushing.

Recommended Goal

A good run should look active and confident. Strong eliminations and high placement matter, but so does clearly showing that mobility was part of your game plan.

Submission Tip

Clip moments where you used movement in fights. That is the best kind of proof for a challenge built around fast repositioning and pressure.
