Grey Loot Only

Use only common or starter-tier gear for the full match. This challenge tests mechanical skill, positioning, and your ability to win with weaker equipment.

Submit Your Run Back to Challenges

Challenge Overview

You are allowed to pick up loot, but only common items can be used. Better gear may be tempting, but the entire point is proving you can still compete without it.

Rules

Only grey/common gear can be used for the match.

Do not swap to better rarity weapons or items.

If you accidentally pick one up, stop using it immediately.

Best Strategy

Since your gear is weaker, you need better aim, smarter timing, and stronger positioning. Focus on controlling fights rather than forcing them.

What Makes It Hard

Most opponents will outgear you, so you cannot rely on raw damage or stronger loot. You have to win by playing cleaner and making fewer mistakes.

Recommended Goal

A strong run here is about proving consistency. High placement, solid eliminations, and fully following the rule set make the run stand out.

Submission Tip

Be sure your proof clearly shows that you stayed on grey loot. That is what makes this challenge believable and competitive on the leaderboard.
