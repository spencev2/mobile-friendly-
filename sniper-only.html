Sniper Only

Long-range precision only. This challenge is built around patience, aim, and discipline rather than close-range panic fighting.

Submit Your Run Back to Challenges

Challenge Overview

Every elimination has to come through accuracy and timing. This challenge rewards calm aim, clean positioning, and smarter engagement distance.

Rules

Use sniper-style weapons as your main source of damage.

Do not rely on close-range spray weapons to finish fights.

Play the challenge honestly from start to finish.

Best Strategy

Keep distance, control height when possible, and avoid letting fights collapse into chaos. The more controlled the fight is, the better this challenge works.

What Makes It Hard

Long-range loadouts punish missed shots and bad timing. You need strong awareness and confidence to make this challenge work consistently.

Recommended Goal

A strong sniper-only run should show both placement and quality eliminations. Big shots, smart positioning, and consistency are what make it impressive.

Submission Tip

Clips work especially well here. A clean long-range elimination or a well-played endgame is the best kind of proof for this challenge.
