One-Spot Loot

Choose one landing or loot area, gather what you can, and never go back. This challenge rewards efficient looting and smarter resource management.

Submit Your Run Back to Challenges

Challenge Overview

Your whole match depends on how well you loot at the start. Once you leave your chosen spot, you cannot return for backup items or better gear.

Rules

Choose one landing or loot area at the start.

Loot only that location in the opening phase.

After leaving, do not return for more supplies.

Best Strategy

Pick a location that gives you balanced resources, then rotate with purpose. Since your setup is limited, every early choice matters more.

What Makes It Hard

If your early loot is weak, you have to make it work. This challenge is less about luck and more about adapting once your options become limited.

Recommended Goal

Try to prove that one clean setup was enough to carry you deep into the match. Strong placement with a solid elimination count makes this challenge stand out.

Submission Tip

Make sure your proof clearly shows the route or the opening loot phase so it is obvious you committed to one area instead of constantly re-looting.
