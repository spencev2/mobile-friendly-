No-Heal Run

Complete your match without using any healing items at all.

Overview

This challenge forces you to rely on smart positioning and clean fights instead of recovery.

Rules

No healing items allowed at any point during the match.

Strategy

Avoid early fights, play edge zones, and only engage when you have an advantage.

Goal

Finish with strong placement and eliminations without ever healing.
