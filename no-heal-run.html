No-Heal Run

Complete your match without using any healing items at all. This challenge rewards careful decision-making, strong positioning, and clean fights.

Submit Your Run Back to Challenges

Challenge Overview

In this challenge, once you take damage, you cannot recover any of that lost health or shields. Every fight matters more, and every mistake becomes harder to recover from.

Rules

No medkits, bandages, shields, slurp items, or any healing sources.

You must follow the rule for the entire match from start to finish.

Your run only counts if you complete the challenge fully.

Best Strategy

Avoid unnecessary early fights, rotate smart, and only commit to battles where you have a clear advantage. This is one of the best challenges for improving discipline.

What Makes It Hard

One bad fight can completely change the rest of the match. Because you cannot heal, every bit of damage stays with you and puts more pressure on later decisions.

Recommended Goal

Try to finish with strong placement while still getting eliminations. A good run is not just survival — it is surviving while still performing well under pressure.

Submission Tip

When you submit, include your placement, eliminations, and proof. This helps make the leaderboard more competitive and makes your run easier to verify.
