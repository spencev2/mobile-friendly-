Minimal Inventory

Limit your inventory and survive with fewer choices. This challenge makes item management, priorities, and discipline way more important.

Submit Your Run Back to Challenges

Challenge Overview

Instead of carrying everything you want, you are forced to survive with less. This challenge makes you commit to a tighter loadout and better overall decision-making.

Rules

Limit yourself to a reduced number of item slots.

Do not fill your inventory completely just because you can.

Stick to the reduced loadout for the full run.

Best Strategy

Choose items that are flexible and useful in multiple situations. Since your inventory is limited, every slot should serve a clear purpose.

What Makes It Hard

Most players rely on inventory depth for backup options. This challenge removes that safety net and forces better planning before things go wrong.

Recommended Goal

The best runs show efficiency. High placement and controlled eliminations matter more when you did it all with fewer tools than everyone else.

Submission Tip

Try to show your reduced inventory clearly in your proof. That instantly makes the run more convincing and helps it stand out as a legit challenge completion.
