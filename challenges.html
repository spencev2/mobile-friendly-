Gaming Challenges

Try one of these challenge ideas to make your matches more intense, more creative, and way more fun.

Featured Challenge Ideas

Pick a challenge, try it, and submit your result once you finish.

No-Heal Run

Finish your match without using any healing items at all. Survive using positioning, smarter fights, and cleaner rotations.

Grey Loot Only

Only use common or starter-tier loot. If you find something better, you have to ignore it and work with the worst gear possible.

Movement Madness

Carry movement items whenever possible and use them aggressively in every fight to stay unpredictable.

One-Spot Loot

Choose one landing spot. Once you leave, you cannot go back. Make your early loot count.

No Reload Challenge

Only reload when completely safe. No panic reloads during fights. This forces smarter timing and better ammo management.

Sniper Only

All of your damage has to come from long-range weapons. Focus on patience, positioning, and landing accurate shots.

Storm Edge Play

Stay near the storm edge instead of rotating to the center early. This tests awareness, timing, and smart movement.

Minimal Inventory

Limit your inventory slots and make every item count. You have to survive with fewer options and smarter choices.

How to Complete a Challenge

Pick one challenge, track how you did, and submit your result after the match.

Step 1: Pick a Challenge

Choose the challenge that looks the most fun or the most difficult depending on what kind of match you want.

Step 2: Play by the Rules

Stick to the challenge rules for the full game and see how well you can adapt under pressure.

Step 3: Submit Your Result

Once you finish, go to the Submit page and enter your challenge result so it can be added later.
