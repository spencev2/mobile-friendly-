Gaming Challenges

Try one of these challenge ideas to make your matches more intense, more creative, and way more fun. Push your skills, test your decision-making, and see how far you can go.

Featured Challenge Ideas

Pick a challenge, try it in your favorite game, and submit your result once you finish.

No-Heal Run

Finish your match without using any healing items at all. You have to survive using positioning, smarter fights, and cleaner rotations.

Grey Loot Only

Only use common or starter-tier loot for the entire game. If you find something better, you have to ignore it and work with the worst gear possible.

Movement Madness

Carry movement items whenever possible and use them aggressively in every fight. The goal is to stay unpredictable and keep the action fast.

One-Spot Loot

Choose one landing spot or one loot area. Once you leave it, you cannot go back for more items. You have to make your early loot count.

No Reload Challenge

You can only reload when you are completely safe. That means no panic reloads during fights and no careless ammo management.

Sniper or Nothing

Your main damage has to come from long-range shots. This challenge forces patience, accuracy, and smarter positioning before every engagement.

Storm Edge Survivor

Play every rotation near the storm edge instead of rushing center zone. This tests survival skills, awareness, and smart timing.

Minimal Inventory

Use only a limited number of inventory slots the whole match. You have to choose carefully and make fewer items do more work.

No Build Panic

If you play a game with building or defensive tools, you cannot instantly spam them the moment you get shot. Stay calm and make every move count.

Silent Run

Avoid drawing attention by limiting unnecessary shots, noise, or risky pushes. The goal is to survive longer using stealth and patience.

Hot Drop Test

Land in the busiest area on the map and survive the early chaos. This is a great challenge for improving fast decision-making under pressure.

Low Ammo Challenge

Limit yourself to a small ammo count and make every shot matter. Wasted bullets make the rest of the match way harder.

How to Complete a Challenge

Pick one challenge, try it in-game, track how you did, and then submit your best result to the site once you are finished.

Step 1: Choose a Challenge

Pick the one that sounds the most fun or the most difficult depending on what kind of experience you want.

Step 2: Track Your Run

Keep note of things like placement, eliminations, survival time, or whether you actually completed all the challenge rules.

Step 3: Submit Your Result

Once you finish, head to the Submit page and enter your challenge result so it can be added to the site later.
