Storm Edge Play

Stay near the storm edge instead of rushing center zone. This challenge is all about timing, awareness, and smart pathing.

Submit Your Run Back to Challenges

Challenge Overview

Instead of playing for center control, you stay near the outer edge and rely on timing and safer rotations to survive deeper into the match.

Rules

Do not rotate deep center early unless absolutely forced.

Stay near the edge and time your movement carefully.

Use the edge as part of your game plan, not as an accident.

Best Strategy

Watch timing closely, avoid getting pinched, and look for safe late rotations. Patience matters more here than rushing every zone.

What Makes It Hard

The storm edge can be safer, but it can also trap you if you move too late. This challenge punishes hesitation and bad timing.

Recommended Goal

A strong run should show that your zone pathing was deliberate and effective. Surviving while still getting meaningful eliminations makes this challenge especially impressive.

Submission Tip

Include clips or proof that show your rotations, especially in later zones. That helps prove you actually committed to edge play instead of just ending there by chance.
